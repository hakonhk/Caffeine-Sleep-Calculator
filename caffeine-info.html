Understanding Caffeine and Sleep

Caffeine is a stimulant that temporarily boosts focus and energy but can interfere with restful sleep when consumed too late in the day.

How Caffeine Affects Your Body

Caffeine’s half-life is around 5-6 hours, meaning half of what you consume can still be in your system by bedtime if you don’t time it well.

Tips for Balancing Caffeine & Sleep

  • Avoid consuming caffeine within 4-8 hours before bedtime.
  • Opt for decaf or herbal teas in the afternoon and evening.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a regular sleep routine.
